Visualizations and Insights

Quantity Ordered Based on Hours (Line Chart):
Shows hourly order trends from 6 AM to 8 PM.
Peak orders occur in the morning hours (around 7-10 AM), with a gradual decline throughout the day.
Insight: Focus marketing or staffing during morning rush hours for better efficiency.

Insight: Balanced distribution across sizes; consider promotions for larger sizes to boost average order value.
